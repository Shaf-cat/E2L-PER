E2L Programme – Partner Engagement Roadmap
From First Conversation to Certified Learners

The E2L programme follows a structured seven-phase journey from market scoping through to certified outcomes and impact reporting. Each phase must be completed before the next begins in full. Phases 1 and 2 are critical gating steps — no programme build activity should begin until partnership formalisation is substantially complete.

Start · Market Interest
Goal · Certified Learners
1
Phase One · 4–8 Weeks
Market Scoping & Partnership Formation
Details & Responsibilities

The foundation of everything. Viizard VLSS brings the programme framework, platform expertise, and curriculum methodology. The Local Delivery Partner brings on-the-ground market knowledge, community relationships, and an understanding of the local landscape that no external organisation could replicate. Together, both parties establish a clear, evidence-based rationale for the programme before any other work begins.

Viizard VLSS
Provide the programme framework, sector scope guidance, and comparative intelligence drawn from Viizard VLSS platform experience across markets and disciplines
Share relevant platform and programme intelligence to inform market scoping, sector selection, and learner demographic decisions
Advise on learner segmentation methodology and review final report
Local Delivery Partner
Lead all on-ground research, surveys, and stakeholder interviews
Map existing provision and identify employer partners
Validate findings through community and government relationships
Phase Complete When
Market assessment report signed off by both parties. Priority sectors and learner segments formally agreed.
2
Phase Two · 4–6 Weeks
Partnership Formalisation
Nothing that follows is possible without this
Details & Responsibilities

The legal and governance relationship between both parties is formally agreed, documented, and signed. No programme build activity should begin until this phase is substantially complete.

Viizard VLSS
Provide standard partnership term sheet as the starting document
Lead on platform, curriculum, and accreditation governance clauses
Draft data processing agreement covering UK GDPR obligations
Local Delivery Partner
Provide all organisational governance documentation
Instruct local legal counsel to review and approve the agreement
Obtain board or trustee resolution authorising the partnership
Phase Complete When
Signed partnership agreement and data processing agreement in place. Both leads formally appointed.
3
Phase Three · 6–10 Weeks
Programme Design & Curriculum
Follows Phase 2
Details & Responsibilities

The programme curriculum is designed to address the skills gaps identified in Phase 1 and meet the needs of the target learner demographic. Viizard VLSS leads the development process, with the Local Delivery Partner contributing local knowledge and context throughout. Where a partner brings an existing programme or curriculum, Viizard VLSS will work collaboratively to assess, align, and configure it appropriately for platform delivery.

Viizard VLSS
Lead curriculum development, quality assurance, and accreditation alignment, ensuring all content meets the standards required for certification
Develop and maintain all lesson plan frameworks, assessment strategy, and educator guidance materials
Review and adapt partner-contributed content where applicable, ensuring it meets platform and accreditation requirements
Local Delivery Partner
Provide local sector context, cultural references, and community-specific case studies to inform and enrich the curriculum
Review all content for local accuracy, relevance, and appropriateness
Confirm learner literacy levels, language requirements, and any specific learning needs within the target demographic
Phase Complete When
Programme curriculum, all lesson plan frameworks, assessment strategy, and educator materials complete and approved by both parties.
4
Phase Four · 4–8 Weeks
Platform Readiness
Parallel with Phase 3
Details & Responsibilities

The Viizard VLSS platform is configured and made fully ready for programme delivery. Every element of the learner experience — from registration and onboarding through to content access, assessment, and progress tracking — is set up, tested, and confirmed before the first learner joins. Mobile optimisation is a standard requirement, as smartphone access is the primary means of participation in most markets.

Viizard VLSS
Configure and prepare the full platform environment for the specific programme, ensuring all learner-facing functions are operational and tested prior to launch
Set up all assessment, submission, progress tracking, and communication tools within the platform
Produce clear guidance materials for learners, educators, and administrators, and provide hands-on training to the Local Delivery Partner's team
Local Delivery Partner
Provide localisation input covering language, terminology, and imagery to ensure the platform feels relevant and accessible to the target learner community
Walk through the full learner journey on the platform and confirm it meets local expectations before launch, raising any concerns with Viizard VLSS in good time
Phase Complete When
Platform fully configured and ready for learners. The complete journey from registration through to certification has been walked through and confirmed by both parties.
5
Phase Five · 4–6 Weeks
Recruitment & Selection
Follows Phase 4
Details & Responsibilities

The application process is launched. Viizard VLSS handles all applicant intake, management, and tracking through its platform, ensuring a consistent and secure experience from first submission through to selection. The Local Delivery Partner leads community outreach, distributes the application form, and drives submissions. A fair, transparent selection process is run and all applicants are notified of outcomes.

Viizard VLSS
Manage and oversee all applicant intake, tracking, and data through the Viizard VLSS platform, ensuring a secure, consistent, and reliable process from end to end
Provide the application form ready for the Local Delivery Partner to distribute across all outreach channels
Develop all standardised applicant communication templates and manage the full selection and notification process
Local Delivery Partner
Lead all community outreach and marketing activity across target learner segments, utilising every channel, network, and relationship available to them as they deem appropriate
Distribute the application form widely across referral networks, community organisations, schools, employers, government agencies, faith groups, and any other avenue likely to attract the right candidates in the right numbers
Provide in-person application support for learners with limited digital access and ensure completed forms are submitted to Viizard VLSS
Phase Complete When
Application process closed. Selection complete. All applicants notified of outcomes. Confirmed cohort ready for onboarding.
6
Phase Six · Programme Duration
Live Programme Delivery
The live programme
Details & Responsibilities

The live programme runs. Viizard VLSS oversees the full learning cycle from enrolment through to final outcomes, with quality assurance embedded throughout the platform. The Local Delivery Partner supports learner engagement and on-ground operations for the duration of delivery.

Viizard VLSS
Oversee the entire learning cycle — from enrolment and content delivery through to assessment, progression, and final outcomes
Monitor learner engagement and performance continuously through platform analytics
Manage all platform-side technical operations and uptime throughout delivery
Hold final approval on educator appointments in conjunction with the accrediting body and certifying institution
Local Delivery Partner
Shortlist and recommend potential educators who are subject-matter experts in the relevant field and, where delivery is in a language other than English, are able to communicate effectively in that language
Operate a local learner helpdesk, working in support of the virtual assistance provided by the Viizard VLSS platform
Lead community engagement activities to sustain learner motivation and attendance throughout the programme
Phase Complete When
Live programme delivered in full. Learner attendance, engagement, and assessment data recorded. End-of-programme quality report produced and shared.
7
Phase Seven · 4 Weeks
Certification & Evaluation
Post Programme Completion
Details & Responsibilities

Certificates are issued, impact data is collected, and the full end-of-cohort review is conducted. A decision is made on the next cohort — the winning condition for the programme.

Viizard VLSS
Issue all certificates and digital credentials to qualifying learners
Produce consolidated programme impact report and funder and stakeholder reporting formats
Lead cross-market comparative reporting where applicable
Local Delivery Partner
Conduct learner follow-up surveys on employment and further study outcomes
Collect case studies and testimonials from learners and community
Present impact evidence to funders, government, and stakeholders
Phase Complete When
End-of-cohort impact report published. Funder and government reports submitted. Decision made on next cohort.

Goal Achieved — Certified Learners & Evidenced Impact

When all seven phases are complete, learners hold accredited qualifications and the programme's contribution to society is evidenced and measurable. Post-programme outcomes — including career progression, new income streams, enterprise formation, improved professional practice, and measurable performance gains — are tracked, reported, and placed on record. All stakeholder obligations are fulfilled and impact evidence is shared across the full partnership ecosystem. The programme does not end — it scales to the next cohort, reaching more lives, more communities, and more markets.
